Alexandria Library

Overview

The Alexandria Library, also known as Bibliotheca Alexandrina, is a modern tribute to the legendary Library of Alexandria, one of the ancient world's most significant centers of learning and culture. It is located along the Mediterranean coast in Alexandria, Egypt.

Why Visit?

  • Explore a world-class cultural and educational center.
  • Marvel at the stunning architectural design, featuring a massive tilted disk.
  • Visit the planetarium, museums, and art galleries housed within the library complex.
  • Discover rare manuscripts and books in one of the world's largest reading spaces.
